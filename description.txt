Wiita's Mod is a fairly cool mod that adds content to the base game
The following stuff is:

- Hamis (enemy) and a pet for it from Noita :) 
- 4 new bows
- 2 new Flamelasers
- A post-plantera sandgun upgrade
- 1 new summoner weapon
- 2 new accessories
